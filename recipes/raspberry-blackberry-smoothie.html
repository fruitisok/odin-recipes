Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie

Description

A delicious berry smoothie. Perfect for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 6 ounces vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Serve into a glass and enjoy!
